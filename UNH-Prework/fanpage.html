Dispatch

Biography: Dispatch formed at Middlebury College in Vermont in the 1990's. To the tune of rock, reggae and folk, the band played concerts at breweries and pubs throughout the early 2000's. They began to play big shows and evlolved into a national name. Dispatch is a New England favorite; perfect for family road trips, backyard summer parties, or simply floating down the river in the summer.
